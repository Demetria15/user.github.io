About

My story

Living in Stockholm, often called the Venice of the North, but born and grown up in the beautiful historical city Vienna. I graduated from the University of Vienna with a Bachelor in Linguistics and hold a diploma in tourism. Working closely with people through different works made me good at observing people’s needs and desires and directed me into UX design.

In September 2020, I decided to take the next step in my career. Working in customer support for several years, I wanted to create user-friendly products and craft my ideas. I am currently working at Klarna as a Frontend designer, designing and implementing communication send-outs, and working in a product team with different competencies.
